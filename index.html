Leadership

We are turnaround specialists: a very close-knit team of former CEOs, CROs, CIOs, and SVPs who have worked together inside high-performance software environments, delivering top-percentile profitability across large B2B portfolios we owned.

For this team, turnarounds are not isolated restructurings; they are disciplined restorations of durable software solutions into long-term, cash-generating assets. The result is a portfolio of companies sustaining 50%+ net margins for years — often decades — after revival.

Operating Framework

We acquire and rehabilitate software companies in the $10–20 million revenue range, rebuilding them into durable cash-flow operations, through direct ownership and active management.

We hold 100% equity and control of each investment and operate without leverage or passive oversight. We fund and manage our portfolio companies directly, supporting them through a centralized shared-services platform.

Precision Acquisitions

Unmatched Speed. Precise. Repeatable. Our proprietary AI platform compresses weeks of sourcing and diligence into minutes. Decades of operating experience are baked into every filter, flag, and signal we run.

Operational Excellence

Revival at Scale. We don't advise—we rebuild. Prytaneum Partners replaces complexity with operational precision, restructuring every company for margin, sustainability, and long-term performance.

Value Creation

Total Control. From code to culture, we install AI-Native systems that produce sustainable margins, repeatable playbooks, and operational precision.

What Our Customers Can Expect

When we revive a business, our priority is continuity—protecting the service, product, and support our customers rely on, while building for their long-term success.

Stability where it matters most

A stronger financial foundation designed to support long-term service continuity.

When we acquire a business, we replace a distressed financial model with a stable operating foundation. This revival is led by an experienced team focused on long-term stewardship—maintaining continuity, improving reliability, and ensuring the product customers depend on remains supported and improved over time.

Renewed focus on customer value

A disciplined product roadmap shaped around the existing customer base.

Revival creates the opportunity to invest in our customers who rely on the product. Our engineering efforts return to what matters most: delivering value to current customers. Roadmap priorities are clarified, distractions are reduced, and customer input plays a central role in shaping what gets built and improved next.

Predictable service continuity

Support, uptime, and responsiveness treated as core operating priorities.

Our revival process is designed to preserve continuity first. Transitions are managed carefully to maintain service levels, with support, uptime, and responsiveness treated as essential—not optional—throughout the revival and beyond.
