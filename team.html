The Prytaneum Team

Meet the experienced professionals driving Prytaneum Partners' success in private equity and software company revival.

Chris Morino

Global PE & AI Leadership | Scaling Profitable Software Companies

Managing & General Partner

Chris Morino is the Managing & General Partner of Prytaneum Partners. He has spent his career building, operating, and scaling software businesses across multiple verticals, from early-stage platforms to global enterprise operations.

Chris has led global software organizations, overseeing multi-company portfolios with hundreds of millions in revenue. In prior operating roles, he delivered sustained operating margins ranging from 75% to over 84% across complex, distressed software portfolios. His work centers on transforming underperforming software companies into durable, high-margin cash generators through disciplined operating models, centralized shared services, and AI-native execution.

Mark Sampson

Private Equity Investor & Advisor | Middle Market Growth Partner

Senior Partner, Capital & Investor Relations

Mark Sampson is Senior Partner, Capital & Fund Operations at Prytaneum Partners. He brings deep experience as a General Partner, having successfully raised and operated multiple private equity funds across market cycles.

As a GP, Mark has led capital raises across five private equity funds, navigating fund formation, first close, final close, and full fund lifecycles. He has spent his career structuring, capitalizing, and operating investment vehicles for middle-market private equity strategies, providing Prytaneum with institutional-grade financial oversight and execution discipline.

At Prytaneum, Mark is responsible for capital strategy, LP engagement, and fund-level operations. He oversees fundraising execution, financial structuring, reporting rigor, and operational controls across the firm's fund platform, ensuring capital is governed with precision, transparency, and durability.

Ian Miller, MBE, CA

Strategic Advisor | Finance & Operations Executive

Senior Partner, Europe & Governance

Ian serves as Senior Partner, Europe & Governance. He is a Chartered Accountant with over 30 years of experience spanning strategy, M&A, performance improvement, and complex business turnarounds across Europe and North America. He spent 14 years in strategy consulting and M&A, including serving as Global Practice Head for his sector, before leading multiple operational transformations as CEO, Chairman, and Executive Vice President. His senior leadership experience includes roles at EDS, Hewlett-Packard, NTT Data, and two publicly listed companies on the London Stock Exchange. Ian brings deep expertise in corporate finance and operations across insurance, healthcare, banking, and capital markets, and was appointed a Member of the Order of the British Empire (MBE).

Jeff Moyer

Technology Executive & Founder | AI-Driven SDLC

Chief Information Officer

Jeff serves as Chief Information Officer. He has previously partnered with the General Partners to lead delivery optimization and cost rationalization across a venture-managed portfolio, achieving outsized gains in operational quality and cost efficiency. With over 30 years of experience, he has held senior leadership roles at EDS, Hewlett-Packard, and DXC Technology, where he applied emerging technologies and scaled operating models across complex application and infrastructure environments. Jeff is an Advisor Member at Furman University for their Strategic AI Program.
